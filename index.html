About Me

As a recent graduate of the Architecture program from University of Minnesota, I am applying my knowledge and interests in design as a store planner for Target. As a store planner, I work with teams of designers, architects, and facilities management to update Targets’ stores as the company grows and evolves to best serve its guests.

I’m currently pursuing a certification in full stack development through the Continued Education Department of the University of Minnesota so I can further integrate my passions for design and technology. I am looking to switch roles into something related to this program and apply my strengths from my previous experiences as a designer.

Aside from my professional career, I enjoy fostering dogs for Midwest Animal Rescue as well as working around cars as a part-time receptionist for Luther Volkswagen.
